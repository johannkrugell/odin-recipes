Licked-Lollipop

Your toddlers behaviour was excellent today! What is a temper tantrum anyways? Time to reward that good behaviour with a lollipop. Every toddler knows that a lollipop tastes better when it was licked by someone else first.

Ingredients

  • A store bought lollipop
  • A sybling or pet
  • Any hard surface

Steps

  1. Remove the lollipop wrapper
  2. Let your pet dog or cat have a few licks
  3. If you don't have a pet try a sybling
  4. Only child? Don't worry...lick the lollipop yourself or just roll it under the fridge
  5. Your done. Your toddler can now enjoy the lollipop just the way he likes it

Bon Appetit!
